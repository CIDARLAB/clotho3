Adding Functionality

There are two primary ways of adding functionality to the angular stack after the app has been bootstrapped: Mixins or Bootstrapping.

Mixins we'll go through first. Mixing in components means lazy-adding them (which is not supported natively by angular - so it's a bit of a hack). You can mix in controllers, filters, services, directives, and routes. You can also transclude templates.

Once a component is mixed-in, it's in. We'll go into more detail each step.

Bootstrapping a new app means that a new angular app is loaded into the page, and is autonomous from the parent app (i.e. what you see now). Bootstrapping is explained in more detail later.
