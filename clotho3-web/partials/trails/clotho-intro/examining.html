Construction Files define a series of unit operations which constitute a cloning experiment.

A simple workflow involves the amplification of your gene of interest via PCR, and digesting it to give it specific sticky ends on each side. A plasmid backbone is cut with restriction enzymes to yield ends complimentary to those of the fragment containing your gene of interest, and a ligation adds your fragment to the backbone. Your final product could then be transformed into bacteria, which would synthesize your gene of interest.
